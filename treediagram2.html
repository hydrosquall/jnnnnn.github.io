Pleasing Variation

Don't forget about these possible variations:
  • Timing (triple step, reverse, hesitation, double-time, slow-quick-quick, quick-quick-slow, wrong way)
  • Translation up/forward/sideways (squat, offset, lean back, slide, sway, flick, close/open hold)
  • Rotation around/forward/sideways (close/wrap, spinning, dips, twisted)
  • Body part (frame/feet/knees/hips/butt/chest/shoulders/head/hands)
